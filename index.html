Mitski

The indie rock star.


I got to know Mitski by an accident, really. One day I was listening to my spotify playlist, and since I am broke, random music started popping out. That's when I heard "Me and My Husband"

I didn't know that, this song would change my whole life, and music taste. Mitski really helped me through difficult times, and were here, when everyone left me.

I am making this page as tribute. I know she won't probably ever see this, but i just felt like i need to.

Mitski - the timeline.

- Mitski was born at September 27th, 1990.


- Mitski began college in film, at Hunter's College.

She transformed to SUNY Purchase College short after, and began study in studio composition.


- January 31th, 2012, Mitski self-released Lush

It was her student project, at before mentioned SUNY Purchase College.


- August 1st, 2013 - Mitski self-released Retired from Sad, New Career In Business

It was her senior year project. The last of projects for college in here.


- November 11th, 2014 - Mitski released Bury Me At Makeout Creek

She released the album under Double Double Whammy, and it was her first album under an label.


The album got re-released in 2015, with 4 bonus tracks, under Don Giovanni Records.


- December 22th, 2015 - Mitski gets signed in Dead Oceans

She continued to work with them for the rest of her career.


- June 17th, 2016 - Mitski released Puberty 2

She took a hiatus from making music after this album. She said this album brought her back to her roots.

Also, it was the first Mitski's album to be released under Dead Oceans.


- August 17th 2018 - Mitski released Be The Cowboy

This is the album i originally got to know Mitski from. The album was really good acclaimed by critics, getting titles of the AOTY(album of the year) by

Pitchfork, Vulture and Consequence of Sound

After the Be The Cowboy Tour ended, Mitski said that she is stopping doing concerts, and at one point she even wanted to quit music.


- February 4th 2022 - Mitski released Laurel Hell.


- 23th of July, 2023 - Mitski announced her 7th studio album via her newsletter - The Land Is Inhospitable And So Are We

We are still waiting for its release. It is speculated to be released this autumn.
